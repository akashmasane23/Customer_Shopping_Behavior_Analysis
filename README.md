## ðŸ“Š Key Questions Addressed

This analysis answers the following business questions:

1. Total revenue generated by male vs. female customers  
2. Customers who used discounts but spent above average  
3. Top 5 products with the highest average review rating  
4. Compare average purchase amount between Standard vs Express shipping  
5. Subscribers vs non-subscribers: average spend & total revenue  
6. Top 5 products with highest percentage of discounted purchases  
7. Customer segmentation: New, Returning, Loyal  
8. Top 3 most purchased products within each category  
9. Are repeat buyers (>5 previous purchases) more likely to subscribe?  
10. Revenue contribution of each age group
